Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order? +

You can track your order by going to the "My Orders" section and selecting the order you want to track.

What is the return policy? +

Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of receipt. Please ensure the items are in their original condition.

How do I update my address? +

You can update your address by visiting the "Manage Address" section and editing or adding a new address.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and UPI payments.

Can I cancel my order? +

Yes, you can cancel your order if it has not been shipped. Go to the "My Orders" section and click on "Cancel Order" for the respective order.
